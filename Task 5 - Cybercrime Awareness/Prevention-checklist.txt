Stay Safe Online


1. Always use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (avoid using the same password everywhere).
2. Enable two-factor authentication on email, banking, and social media accounts.
3. Always verify website URLs before entering personal or login details.
4. Keep your mobile apps and system updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
6. Never share OTP or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
7. Do not click on suspicious links received through SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
8.  Do not approve unknown UPI collect requests (Pay requests).
9. Do not scan random QR codes to receive money (you only scan to pay).
10. Immediately block your bank account or UPI if you suspect fraud.
