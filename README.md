# **Digital Literacy Project**

## **📌 Overview**

This project focuses on developing essential digital skills including digital literacy, professional communication, data analysis, and cyber safety. It consists of five tasks designed to improve understanding of responsible and effective use of technology.

---

## **📂 Project Structure**

* task-1-presentation/  
* task-2-online-presence/  
* task-3-data-analysis/  
* task-4-email-etiquette/  
* task-5-cybercrime/

---

## **🔹 Task 1 – Digital Literacy**

Created an infographic presentation explaining digital literacy, its importance, and safe internet practices.

---

## **🔹 Task 2 – Online Presence**

Created professional accounts on LinkedIn, GitHub, and Kaggle. Also designed a Google Form quiz and collected responses.

---

## **🔹 Task 3 – Data Analysis**

Analyzed given data, identified key observations, and drew meaningful conclusions.

---

## **🔹 Task 4 – Email Etiquette**

Wrote professional emails and created a social media do’s and don’ts checklist to understand proper digital communication.

---

## **🔹 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness**

Learned about cybercrime types, created a prevention checklist, and understood reporting methods such as the National Cyber Crime Portal and helpline 1930\.

---

## **📌 Key Learnings**

* Importance of digital literacy  
* Professional online presence  
* Data interpretation skills  
* Email and communication etiquette  
* Cyber safety and awareness

---

## **📎 Conclusion**

This project helped in understanding how to use digital tools safely, responsibly, and effectively in both personal and professional life.
